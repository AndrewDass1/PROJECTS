You are Angry

It seems you express a lot of frustation when talking to people or in many situations. While it is okay to express frustation, its best to take steps to control your anger. It is not a good trait to easily be agitated by many people or in many sitations. Showing a temper would make others avoid you since they do not want to be around an angry person. Some advice to not become as angry is understand why a person said something that made you rage or something that occurred in a certain situation that made you rage. By thinking positively from another perspective, the person that angered you probably did not intend to make you rage but instead give advice. If a certain event occurred that you disliked, think about why it happened and also take next steps to prevent a similar event in the future from happening.
