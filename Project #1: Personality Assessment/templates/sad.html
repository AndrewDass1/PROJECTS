You are Sad

It is okay to be sad. Every person has their good and bad days. We want to avoid having bad days because it can cause us to be depressed. Being sad for many days however, is a problem since it can impact your confidence, mood and social life. Despite what you are going through, always remember to always try your best to be the best person you can be! People such as your family or friends are counting on you!
